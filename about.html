A B O U T   U S

Lens photogrpahy club aims to build a community of students who share common interests in photography.
The club welcomes all students of various skill levels and different levels of experiences. Members will have access to
workshops,lectures, activities, and competitions. The club also provides hands on experiences on different medium
such as digital photography and film photography. Members will also have access to different kinds
of software for post processing the images.
